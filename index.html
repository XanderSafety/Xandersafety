About Xander Safety

Xander Safety is a field-driven health, safety, and environment (HSE) consulting company providing experienced safety leadership, site coverage, and compliance support to industrial, construction, mining, and energy projects.

We specialize in practical, boots-on-the-ground safety support—not paperwork for the sake of paperwork. Our focus is on helping clients manage risk at the workface, meet regulatory and client requirements, and maintain safe, productive operations without slowing the job down.

What We Do

Xander Safety supplies qualified safety professionals and HSE leadership to support projects of all sizes, from early works and construction through shutdowns, turnarounds, and full operations.

Our Approach

  • Visible in the field — engaged at the workface with crews and supervision
  • Aligned with operations — safety that supports productivity and schedule
  • Communication-first — coaching, mentoring, and clear expectations
  • Focused on critical controls — managing high-risk activities effectively

Xander Safety integrates directly into project teams, providing leadership, mentoring, and real-time risk management. We support supervisors and workers in making safe decisions while maintaining quality and productivity.
