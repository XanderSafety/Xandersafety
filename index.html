What We Do

Xander Safety Ltd. provides practical, boots-on-the-ground safety support focused on controlling real risk at the workface. We integrate directly into project teams to support supervisors, crews, and management in maintaining safe, compliant, and productive operations.

Services

Safety Personnel & Leadership

  • Safety Advisors / Safety Officers – Day and night shift field coverage
  • Senior HSE Leads / Turnaround Safety Managers – Leadership support for shutdowns and high-risk scopes
  • Mine Site Safety Coordinators – On-site coordination and compliance monitoring

Planning, Risk & Compliance

  • Project-Specific HSE Management Plans – Client- and site-aligned safety planning
  • Hazard Identification & Risk Assessment – FLHA, JHA/JSA, THA, and task-based risk reviews
  • Incident Investigation & Corrective Actions – Root cause analysis and follow-up
  • Audit & Regulatory Readiness – COR support and inspection preparation

Our Approach

  • Field visible — active presence where work is performed
  • Operations aligned — safety that supports productivity and schedule
  • Accountable — clear expectations and follow-through
