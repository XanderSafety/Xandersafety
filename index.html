What We Provide

Xander Safety delivers practical, boots-on-the-ground safety support that helps crews work safely, supervisors stay compliant, and projects maintain productivity. We focus on real risk control at the workface—not paperwork for its own sake.

Services

Safety Personnel & Leadership

  • Safety Advisors / Safety Officers – Day & night shift field coverage supporting construction, industrial, and energy projects
  • Senior HSE Leads / Turnaround Safety Managers – Leadership-level HSE management for shutdowns, turnarounds, and high-risk scopes
  • Mine Site Safety Coordinators – On-site safety coordination, compliance monitoring, and workforce engagement for mining operations

Planning, Risk & Compliance

  • Project-Specific HSE Management Plans – Site- and client-aligned plans, expectations, and critical controls
  • Hazard Identification & Risk Assessment – FLHA, JHA/JSA, THA, and task-based risk assessments
  • Incident Investigation & Corrective Actions – Root cause analysis, action tracking, and verification
  • Audit & Regulatory Readiness – COR support, internal audits, and inspection readiness

About Xander Safety

We integrate directly into project teams to strengthen safety performance through visible leadership, coaching, and consistent field presence. Our approach is straightforward: focus on critical risks, communicate clearly, support supervisors, and build practical controls that work in real conditions.

  • Workface-focused — engaged with crews where the risk actually exists
  • Operations-aligned — safety that supports schedule, quality, and productivity
  • Accountable — clear expectations, follow-up, and measurable improvement
